Innovation Bootcamp 2025

Turn your innovative ideas into reality with our intensive 8-week program

About the Bootcamp

Our Bootcamp helps aspiring African innovators turn their ideas into action. The program includes mentorship, prototyping sessions, and funding support for the most promising projects.

Duration

8 weeks (September 1 - October 27, 2025)

Location

Kigali Innovation City, Rwanda (with virtual participation options)

Funding

Top 3 projects receive $10,000 seed funding each

Who Should Apply?

  • African innovators aged 18-35
  • Individuals or teams with innovative ideas
  • Those passionate about solving African challenges
  • No prior technical experience required
